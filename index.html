Founder's Message

A vision built to outgrow
a single classroom

HARIKA NEXUS ACADEMY began as a question: what if a school didn't stop at graduation? Every institution under this roof — laboratory school, coaching, university guidance, career development, and college — is built to hand a student forward to the next, so no learner ever has to start over.

This is our founder's most personal project: a campus ecosystem grown deliberately, one institution at a time, through 2027 to 2029, so each new addition is built with the same care as the first.

Vision To become the region's most trusted end-to-end education ecosystem.
Mission Guide every learner from first classroom to first career, without a single gap.
Core Values Curiosity, integrity, discipline, and craftsmanship in every institution we build.

FAQ

Common questions

Which institution opens first? +

Harika Laboratory School and Harika Edusphere are scheduled to open in 2027, followed by Harika Univision and Harika Career Edge in 2028, and Harika Laboratory College in 2029.

Can I apply to more than one institution? +

Yes. Because all five institutions share one ecosystem, students are encouraged to plan a pathway across multiple stages of the academy.

Is scholarship support available? +

Merit-based scholarships will be available at each institution. Details will be published as each admission cycle opens.

Will there be an online application system? +

Yes. An online admission portal is part of the ecosystem roadmap, alongside a student login, parent portal, and result portal.
